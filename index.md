## BrainStress Quizzes

BrainStress Quizzes is a simple iOS app that's purpose is to work more like a coffee. Open the app, get angry! You can choose between quizzes such as:

1. Math
  - Additions
  - Subtractions
  - Multiplications
  - Divisions
2. Geography
  - Guess the Capital
3. Corporate
  - Guess the Company (motto based)
